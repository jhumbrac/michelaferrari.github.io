Happiness 3

Happiness 3 is a subscription box service, developed alongside a team of 4 students for an entrepreneurship class. It provides college students happiness in small doses, by receiving small curated gifts sent from home. During 10 weeks, we generated (and pivoted from) our concept, established the marketing, financial and launch plans. I was in charge of the visual identity and branding.

Changing The World Via Venture Creation

My team developed a new shopping concept called Fashtag to improve user experience with online shopping. We created a new concept where the user could find shopping items by searching compiled shopping lists, curated by experts and users. The goal was to create a customizable shopping experience based on style, mood and trend, while making the process faster and more efficient. We conducted research through questionnaires and industry analysis. We created our final concept, integrated marketing and expansion plans, as well as a profit-and-loss and risk analysis.
